Wild Rapids Rafting Adventures

Experience the thrill of a lifetime with our expert guides and unforgettable white water rafting adventures in Delta State, Nigeria. We offer a range of trips for all skill levels, from peaceful floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids. Safety is our top priority, and we are committed to providing a memorable and enjoyable experience for everyone.

History

Founded in 2024 by Samuel Justina Onome, a passionate advocate for adventure tourism in Nigeria, our company has quickly become a leading provider of white water rafting experiences. We are dedicated to showcasing the natural beauty of Delta State and providing sustainable tourism opportunities for the local community.
